About Mermaid Swimming Class

Welcome to Mermaid Swimming Class (MSC), where we believe swimming is a vital life skill and a source of endless fun. Founded in 2010, our mission has been to provide high-quality swimming instruction in a safe, encouraging, and positive environment.

Our certified instructors are passionate about teaching and are trained to work with swimmers of all ages and abilities, from nervous beginners to aspiring competitive athletes. We focus on building confidence in the water through a structured, yet fun, curriculum.
